Get the motion subspace matrix, i.e.

Returns the motion subspace matrix

the matrix that maps the joint velocity to the relative twist between the two links. In particular the motion subspace matrix is the S matrix such that v_linkA = S_{linkA,linkB}*dq + linkA_X_linkB*v_linkB where dq is the joint velocity.

Note

The motion subspace matrix is also known in literature as hinge matrix, hinge map matrix, joint map matrix or joint motion map matrix. Get the motion subspace matrix derivatives, i.e. the matrix that maps the joint velocities to the relative spatial acceleration between the two links. In particular the motion subspace matrix derivative is the dS matrix such that a_linkA = S_{linkA,linkB}*ddq + dS_{linkA,linkB}*dq + linkA_X_linkB*a_linkB where ddq is the joint acceleration and dq is the joint velocity.

Note

The motion subspace matrix is also known in literature as hinge matrix, hinge map matrix, joint map matrix or joint motion map matrix. Get the kinematic coupling matrix, i.e. the matrix that maps the joint velocities to the derivative of the joint coordinates.

For the joint whose configuration is in R^n, the kinematic coupling matrix is equal to the identity.
